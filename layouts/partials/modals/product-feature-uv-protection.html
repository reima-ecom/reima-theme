UV protection

With lab tests verified UV sun protection indicates how much longer a child can play in the sun without the delicate skin to burn underneath the clothing. We measure the UV cut using the standard AS/NZS 4399:1996.

UV cut 20+

prevents 95% of ultraviolet radiation.

UV cut 30+

prevents 96,7% of ultraviolet radiation.

UV cut 40+

prevents 97,5% of ultraviolet radiation.

UV cut 50+

prevents 98% of ultraviolet radiation.
